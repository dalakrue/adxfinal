# Lunch Integration

## Closed-first behavior

Fields 1–3 use `All Lunch fields closed` as the first-load state. Selecting a field imports only that renderer and does not calculate.

## Field 1

Table 3 is protected and unchanged. Table 1 maps Net Pressure, Direction Confirmation, and the explicit final production decision from the same run, generation, broker candle, snapshot hash, and signature. Raw labels are preserved.

Table 4 remains production-owned. Its research threshold expander does not alter thresholds and reports insufficient settled evidence rather than manufacturing a result.

Table 5 is a selected identity join of Table 1 and Table 4. `Master Action` is the last production column. Research columns follow a visible boundary and cannot feed back into production.

## Field 2

The Power BI renderer validates the six-field identity and one authoritative completed H1 timestamp. It uses a single projection-state enum and never substitutes a stale path. Failure of the optional green WAIT PULLBACK / HOLD & PROTECT overlay does not remove the protected production chart.

## Field 3

Existing regime displays remain. A closed research expander adds Markov lifecycle data from the separate CRCEF-SV publication.
