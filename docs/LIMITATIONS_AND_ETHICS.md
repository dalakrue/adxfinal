# Limitations and Ethics

- Research outputs are not guaranteed forecasts or trading advice.
- A prediction interval is an empirical uncertainty statement, not a guaranteed future range.
- Small samples, class imbalance, structural breaks, spread/slippage, and data-source revisions can materially change results.
- Attention or feature importance does not prove causality.
- News sentiment cannot be a final directional decision by itself.
- Optional TFT/FinBERT artifacts may be unavailable; the application must continue with explicit `MODEL_UNAVAILABLE` status.
- Live API, MetaTrader, and broker credentials are never embedded in the project.
- Missing historical data is reported; it is not imputed into invented decisions or outcomes.
- No research model is automatically promoted to production.
