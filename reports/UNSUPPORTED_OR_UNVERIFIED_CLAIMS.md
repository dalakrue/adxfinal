# Unsupported or Unverified Claims

The completed package does **not** claim any of the following:

- improved EURUSD direction accuracy;
- improved profitability, Sharpe ratio, TP-hit rate or reduced drawdown;
- proof that any challenger is superior before settled out-of-sample SPA evidence passes all gates;
- guaranteed conformal coverage under arbitrary covariate shift;
- exact reproduction of each paper’s full experimental environment;
- whole-app CPU/RAM improvement from the isolated component benchmark;
- zero cost on every device/browser—only no new research execution on tab/expander paths;
- successful live Streamlit Cloud deployment;
- live-market or broker-data validation;
- direct Python 3.12 execution in this container;
- that the new weights are production-authoritative;
- that statistical significance alone justifies model promotion.

Any future uplift claim must be backed by chronologically settled, purged out-of-sample evidence, calibration checks, important-slice safety, resource budgets, a second evaluation window and rollback readiness.
