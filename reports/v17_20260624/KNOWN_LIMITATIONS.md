# Known limitations

- The execution container lacks the `streamlit` package and executable. Real entry-point import and live startup smoke tests are therefore NOT TESTABLE here.
- The full legacy pytest suite exceeded the available 120-second command window. Targeted relevant suites completed successfully.
- BOCPD, DML, doubly robust and MCS implementations are bounded shadow diagnostics suitable for this project's dependency constraints; they are not claims of full paper reproductions.
- Metrics requiring real matured history remain unavailable/insufficient rather than fabricated.
