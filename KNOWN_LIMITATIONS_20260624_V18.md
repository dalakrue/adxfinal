# Known Limitations

- Research outputs are shadow-only and must not be interpreted as guaranteed trading improvement.
- CQR and calibration explicitly report insufficient evidence on small settled samples.
- The V18 lightweight ensemble uses inexpensive proxies rather than a large neural deep ensemble.
- Browser-level iPhone 11 Pro inspection and live Streamlit startup require installing the declared deployment dependencies.
- The optional existing DuckDB test requires the `duckdb` package already listed in requirements.
