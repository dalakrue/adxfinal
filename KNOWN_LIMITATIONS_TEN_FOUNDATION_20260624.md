# Known Limitations

- The engine is a lightweight operational implementation of the requested research concepts, not a claim of exact replication of every paper's full experimental setup.
- PBO and DSR remain unavailable or provisional until sufficient genuinely independent settled trials exist.
- Regime and changepoint outputs are shadow diagnostics and cannot replace Field 1.
- The live Streamlit server smoke test could not run in the delivery container because Streamlit is not installed there.
- The full legacy test suite exceeded the available execution window after reaching roughly 37% without a reported failure; delivery-critical targeted tests passed.
