# Evidence and Limitations

1. The Field 2 change improves the *definition and calibration boundary* of direction accuracy. It cannot guarantee a higher future accuracy percentage, profitable trades, or lower market loss.
2. Selective actionability can raise accuracy among acted-on forecasts while lowering coverage. Both accuracy and coverage are displayed together.
3. The first few validation rows use a bounded fixed threshold until enough prior ranges exist; no later range is used.
4. Field 6 is display-only. It exposes already-persisted research evidence and does not create new ML features or train a model when opened.
5. The packaged database has no settled rows in the selected 19 future-strategy research tables. `INSUFFICIENT EVIDENCE` is therefore expected until a future valid Settings generation publishes rows.
6. The equation/theorem registry explains formal properties and assumptions. It does not claim that theorem assumptions always hold in EURUSD H1 markets.
7. The AI fact-pack recovery requires a valid completed canonical generation. If both current and last-valid canonical generations are invalid or absent, the original Run Calculation guidance remains correct.
8. No external AI API or new BUY/SELL engine was introduced.
