# Data Dictionary

Every normalized research row uses the canonical run identity and includes or maps to: `run_id`, `origin_id`, `symbol`, `timeframe`, `broker_candle_time`, `data_cutoff`, `source_data_hash/market_data_hash`, `model_version/method_version`, `horizon`, `status`, and creation/maturity identity. Pending and matured evidence remain distinct; unavailable evidence is not fabricated.
