# Field 3 Daily-Lock Methodology

Lower remains responsive under the protected lookback. Middle is evaluated from exactly 120 completed H1 candles; Higher from exactly 600 completed H1 candles. Middle/Higher publish once per broker trading date and remain frozen until the next broker-day boundary. Intraday changes are retained as candidate diagnostics only. Missing/incomplete data preserves the prior valid lock. Historical rows retain the regime published at that historical time.
