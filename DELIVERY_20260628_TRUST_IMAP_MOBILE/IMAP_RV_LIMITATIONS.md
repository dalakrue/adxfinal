# IMAP-RV Limitations

- Prototype rather than complete academic replication.
- Equal-weight research baseline; no validated optimized weights.
- No final untouched holdout results.
- News quality depends on available published history.
- External DXY/yield dependencies are not invented when unavailable.
- Model crowding uses a simplified internal dependence proxy.
- IC inference lacks full HAC/block-bootstrap sensitivity.
- No LPPL, full Matrix Profile, SHAP, conformal, BOCPD, DMA or HSMM implementation was newly completed.
- Score is not a live trade command or profit guarantee.
