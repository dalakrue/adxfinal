# IMAP-RV Feature Dictionary

- `information_value_history`: novelty, relevance, surprise/non-absorption and absorption state.
- `path_memory_history`: recursive short/long trend/activity, path volatility and horizon ranges.
- `model_crowding`: raw confidence, residual correlation, redundancy and independent contribution.
- `signal_validity`: horizon IC, effective sample size, raw/adjusted p-values and grade.
- `beta_fragility`: available Beta magnitude/instability/dependency information; missing dependencies remain missing.
- `raw_evidence_correlation` / `cleaned_evidence_correlation`: raw and PSD-cleaned dependence.
- `diversity_weighted_consensus`: reliability, dependence penalty and normalized contribution.
- `attention_hype`: sentiment-independent attention/novelty/duplication indicators.
- `imap_rv_reliability_decomposition`: normalized component, weight and contribution.
