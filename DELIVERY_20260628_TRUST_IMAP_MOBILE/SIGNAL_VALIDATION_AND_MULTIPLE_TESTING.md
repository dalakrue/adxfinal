# Signal Validation and Multiple Testing

Candidate numeric features are paired with matured H+1, H+3 and H+6 returns. The prototype reports Pearson/Spearman IC, effective sample size, approximate t-statistic, raw p-value, Benjamini–Hochberg adjusted p-value and a research-validity grade.

Constant features are excluded. Effective sample size does not exceed raw rows. The current p-value calculation is an approximation; HAC and block-bootstrap sensitivity analysis, Deflated Sharpe Ratio and PBO remain incomplete.
