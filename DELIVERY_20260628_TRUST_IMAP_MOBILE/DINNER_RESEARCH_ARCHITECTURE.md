# Dinner Research Architecture

IMAP-RV is a separate read-only research envelope. Heavy work runs from Settings; Dinner renders one cached field at a time. It never overwrites the protected Lunch direction.

Fields:

1. Research Snapshot Identity
2. Information Efficiency and Residual Information Value
3. Path-Dependent Volatility Memory
4. Internal Model Crowding and Consensus Fragility
5. Signal IC and Multiple-Testing Validation
6. Effective Breadth and Beta Fragility
7. Cleaned Evidence Covariance
8. Diversity-Weighted Evidence Consensus
9. NLP Attention and Hype
10. IMAP-RV Reliability Decomposition

The envelope is keyed by canonical identity and persisted to a separate SQLite research database. Mobile mode renders at most 10 rows for the selected field; desktop renders up to 50.
