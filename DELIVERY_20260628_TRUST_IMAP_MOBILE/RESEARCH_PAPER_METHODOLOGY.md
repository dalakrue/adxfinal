# Research-Paper Methodology Status

The project retains its existing ARERT/thesis modules and adds IMAP-RV research prototypes. The delivery does not falsely label approximations as full academic replications.

## Implemented research prototypes

- Grossman–Stiglitz-inspired residual information value.
- Guyon–Lekeufack-inspired recursive path memory.
- Internal crowding/redundancy and evidence-diversity analysis.
- Time-series IC with effective sample size and Benjamini–Hochberg adjustment.
- Shrinkage covariance with positive-semidefinite repair.
- Separate sentiment/attention/hype measures.
- Equal-weight IMAP-RV reliability decomposition and four-action protection layer.

## Not completed as full paper implementations

Matrix Profile, multidimensional Matrix Profile, SHAP, adaptive conformal prediction, Bayesian online changepoint detection, dynamic model averaging, full probability calibration, integrated reject-option optimization, SelectiveNet and multivariate HSMM were not newly completed and academically validated in this delivery. Existing project modules may expose related approximations, but they are not claimed here as complete replications.
