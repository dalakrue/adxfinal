# IMAP-RV Theory

IMAP-RV means Information Mining, Attention, Path-Memory and Research Validity. It is a separate reliability decomposition, not a trade direction.

Available normalized components are combined with equal weights over the components that have valid evidence. Raw values, normalized values, applied weights, weighted contributions, source/version metadata and data-quality state are retained.

The score integrates information value, path-memory quality, signal validity, evidence diversity, calibration proxies, crowding fragility, attention instability and multiple-testing risk. Missing components are excluded and disclosed rather than imputed.
